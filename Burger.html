Classic Burger Recipe

Juicy & Flavorful Burger

Our classic burger recipe is everything you want in a burger—juicy, flavorful, and easy to make. Perfect for any occasion, customize it with your favorite toppings for the ultimate burger experience!

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon mustard (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup (optional)
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
Instructions:
  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties, about 3/4 inch thick. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  4. While the burgers cook, toast the buns in a separate skillet or on the grill until golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  5. Assemble the burger: Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each bun, then add the cooked patty with melted cheese, followed by tomato, onion slices, and optional condiments like mustard and ketchup.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately. Enjoy your juicy, flavorful homemade burger!
Customize your burger with pickles, bacon, or any other toppings you love!
