Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 oz (115g) semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (120g) powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter and cocoa powder (for greasing ramekins)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease four ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. In a heatproof bowl, melt the chocolate and butter together. Stir until smooth.
  3. Add powdered sugar to the melted chocolate mixture and whisk until combined.
  4. Beat in the eggs and egg yolks one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gently fold in the flour and a pinch of salt until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the ramekins, filling each about 3/4 full.
  7. Bake for 12–14 minutes. The edges should be firm, but the center slightly jiggly.
  8. Let the cakes cool for 1–2 minutes, then invert onto a plate and serve immediately.

Enjoy your Chocolate Lava Cake with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or fresh berries!
