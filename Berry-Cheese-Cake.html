Berry Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup (60g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 16 oz (450g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy whipping cream
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) berry jam or jelly

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust. Refrigerate while preparing the filling.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture. Add vanilla extract and mix until combined.
  4. Spread the cream cheese filling evenly over the crust. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.
  6. Before serving, top the cheesecake with fresh berries. Warm the berry jam slightly and drizzle over the top for a glossy finish.

Enjoy your creamy and refreshing Berry Cheesecake with a delightful berry topping!
