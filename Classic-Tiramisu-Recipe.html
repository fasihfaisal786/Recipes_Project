Classic Tiramisu Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups (480ml) brewed espresso, cooled
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg yolks and sugar together until thick and pale. Set over a double boiler and whisk for 5-7 minutes until slightly thickened. Let it cool.
  2. Fold the mascarpone cheese into the cooled egg mixture until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Combine the cooled espresso and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish.
  5. Dip each ladyfinger briefly into the coffee mixture and arrange them in a single layer in a 9x13 inch dish.
  6. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  8. Dust the top with cocoa powder before serving.

Enjoy your Classic Tiramisu chilled with a sprinkle of cocoa on top!
