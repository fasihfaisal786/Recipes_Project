Bruschetta

Ingredients: Fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, olive oil, and baguette slices.

Instructions: Dice the tomatoes, chop the basil, and mince the garlic. Mix them together with olive oil and a pinch of salt. Toast the baguette slices and top with the tomato mixture. Serve immediately.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients: Button mushrooms, cream cheese, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, and herbs.

Instructions: Remove the stems from the mushrooms and fill the caps with a mixture of cream cheese, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, and herbs. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes or until golden brown.

Caprese Skewers

Ingredients: Cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella balls, fresh basil, and balsamic glaze.

Instructions: Thread cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil leaves onto skewers. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.
