Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 large cucumber, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup black olives, pitted and sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the cucumber, tomatoes, bell pepper, and red onion.
  2. Add the mixed greens, olives, and crumbled feta cheese on top.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine all ingredients.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy a fresh, healthy salad!
Note: You can customize this salad by adding grilled chicken, nuts, or your favorite salad dressing.
